Corpus Self-Audit

Map your own Claude Code incident log against the three-stage framework. Get a Defense Level recommendation in 2 minutes, grounded in two independent longitudinal corpora.

Free, runs client-side, nothing transmitted. Calibrated against the thecatfix corpus (#62101, 16-month 62-incident log) and the j2clark corpus (#61932, 9-week 7-pass investigation with 228-issue close-rate analysis).
